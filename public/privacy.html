Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Meeting Bingo ("the app") is a real-time multiplayer bingo game for use during meetings. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data we collect

  • Nickname — a display name you choose. It is stored in our database and visible to other players in the same room.
  • Game state — the bingo card assigned to you, which tiles you have marked, and whether you have won. This data is stored only for the duration of the game session.
  • Room data — room codes and word lists created by room hosts.

Data we do not collect

We do not collect email addresses, phone numbers, location data, payment information, or any form of persistent personal profile.

Third-party services

The app uses Google Firebase (Firestore) to store and sync game state in real time. Firebase may collect usage metrics subject to Google's privacy policy.

Data retention

Game rooms and player data are not automatically deleted. Room codes are short-lived in practice — once a room code is lost, the data is no longer accessible to players.

Contact

For questions about this policy, open an issue on the project repository or contact the app owner directly.
